Projects

Our programs span long-baseline monitoring, all-sky spectroscopy, and high-cadence time-domain surveys—designed to measure lags, dynamics, and spectra at scale.

01 • SAMP

SNU AGN Monitoring Project

Long-baseline optical monitoring of nearby AGNs to measure continuum/line lags, map BLR geometry, and calibrate black-hole mass scaling.

Cadence: days–weeks
Bands: ugriz
Targets: nearby Seyferts
Reverberation mapping Open pipelines Public releases

02 • KAMP

KMTNet AGN Monitoring Project

Round-the-clock monitoring using the three KMTNet 1.6-m telescopes to capture high-cadence variability across long baselines.

Cadence: sub-day
Global sites: CTIO, SAAO, SSO
Duration: multi-year
