Projects

Our programs span long-baseline monitoring, all-sky spectroscopy, and high-cadence time-domain surveys—designed to measure lags, dynamics, and spectra at scale.

01 • SAMP

SNU AGN Monitoring Project

Long-term observation to precisely measure the size of BLRs in the AGNs by capturing the variability of the black holes. This study will provide crucial constraints on size-luminosity relation and single-epoch BH mass calibration as well, especially for targets at the high-luminosity end.

Cadence: months
Spectroscopic monitoring
Targets: high-luminosity
Reverberation mapping AGN BLR R-L relation
